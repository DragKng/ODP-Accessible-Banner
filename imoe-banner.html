  • In My Own Eyes: photostories by aboriginal youth

    In Ontario, almost half of Aboriginal people – First Nation, Métis and Inuit – are less than 25 years old. They live on reserves and in towns and cities across the province. This photography project gives Aboriginal youth a voice.

    InMyOwnEyes.ca: Seven Aboriginal communities in Ontario. More than fifty Aboriginal young people. Sharing stories through their own eyes.
